Campus Life

School Auditorium

Ryan Internation school has a huge Auditorium that is located at the 7th floor of the building. The Auditorium has a seating capacity of around 125-150 people.The stage area is of sufficient size to host number of stage sets for musical and dramatic performances. The offstage areas can be readily accessible to adequate dressing room space. The Audtorium also has the rehearsal rooms for chorus, orchestra, and band In close proximity to the stage in order to facilitate the preparation for rehearsals, concerts, and recitals.Mega events like Graduation day, annual day, and many interactive sessions are conducted here.



School library

The School Library is located on the 2nd floor of the building. The library has a seating capacity of 50 students. There is a cheif librarian who looks to the working of library.There are many books in the library. We share the books with our parteners after we finish reading them. It is fun reading in the library.



School Chemistry Lab

The School Chemistry Lab is located at the 3rd floor of the building. It has many burners, diagrams, chemicals and a skeleton.I once visited with my friends in 4th standard and saw the states of matters.

Samson Daniel Award
Samson Daniel Award

Ryan International Group was awarded the prestigious 'Samson Daniel Award' this year for commendable contribution to the cause of the elders of the society, at the awards ceremony organized by HelpAge India on the International Day of Older Persons. The award was presented by Dr. Vinod K Paul, Member NITI Aayog and accepted by Dr. Snehal Pinto, Director, Ryan Group.
